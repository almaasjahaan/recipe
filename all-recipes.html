Chicken & Roasted Red Pepper Roll-Ups

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup roasted red peppers, sliced
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Steps

1: Preheat oven to 190°C.
2: Flatten chicken, layer peppers and cheese, roll up.
3: Sear, bake for 20-25 min.

Risotto with Onions, Bacon & Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 4 strips bacon
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • Parmesan & butter

Steps

1: Cook bacon, sauté mushrooms & onions.
2: Stir in rice, wine, then broth gradually.
3: Add cheese and bacon.

Steamed Crab Legs

Ingredients

  • 1 lb crab legs
  • 1 tbsp Old Bay seasoning
  • 2 tbsp butter, melted
  • 1 lemon, sliced

Steps

1: Steam crab legs with seasoning and lemon.
2: Serve hot with butter dip.

Grilled Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Toppings of choice

Steps

1: Roll out dough, grill one side.
2: Flip, add toppings, grill until cheese melts.

Spicy Pesto Chicken & Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 tbsp pesto
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • Grated parmesan

Steps

1: Cook pasta. Sauté chicken with chili.
2: Mix with pesto, combine all, sprinkle cheese.
